# Tech Stack

In order to provide the best possible SaaS Platform, we need state-of-the-art technologies. Our tech stack enables us to continuously build, test and release new features.

We like to experiment with promising new technologies and working methods. Agile methodologies are in our DNA and there's a lot to tell about our way of working.
